Kyrie Irving

Kyrie Irving was born on March 23, 1992 in Melbourne, Victoria. He is a professional basketball player for the Cleveland Cavaliers. He played high school basketball for St. Patricks School in New Jersey. He then played college basketball at Duke University. He was drafted with the first pick by the Cavaliers in the 2011 NBA draft. He then became the 2011-2012 rookie of the year. He has achieved many accolades, but his most recent accomoplishment is winning the 2016 NBA finals with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Currently, Irving wants to get out fo Cleveland and be traded to another team.
